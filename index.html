The 7th China Summer Workshop on Information Management (CSWIM 2013) will be held in Tianjin, China on June 29 and 30, 2013. It will be hosted by the College of Management and Economics at Tianjin University. The purpose of the workshop is to create a bridge to promote lively exchange and collaborations between information systems scholars in China and other countries, and to advance our understandings of how information systems and technologies affect individuals, businesses, organizations, and societies.  The theme of CSWIM 2013 is “Big Data Analytics and Emerging Applications.”

We welcome submissions of original research papers addressing issues concerning the theory, design, development, evaluation, and application of information systems and management. Since it is a research workshop, CSWIM encourages submissions of research-in-progress that are innovative and thought-provoking. Research articles particularly sought after are those driven by real-world business problems and validated with proper research methodologies.

For a list of topics of interest and submission instructions, see Call for Papers.
